Cub Scouting Pack 413

        Our goal is to prepare our young children for the future ahead of them, and establish a strong foundation that will lead them into becoming strong, independent, respectful, responsible, good and moral leaders for the next generation. We are a family focused organization that follows the principles of the Cub Scout Promise and Law of the Pack and focuses on personal development through fun, adventure, achievement and hands-on learning via structured activities. We focus on the development of positive appreciation for the outdoors and personal fitness. We provide them with opportunities for mental and physical fitness, develop their teamwork and future leadership skills, and provide them with safe, fun and year-round activities. We also teach them outdoor skills, and encourage each Cub Scout to develop and strengthen his faith.
