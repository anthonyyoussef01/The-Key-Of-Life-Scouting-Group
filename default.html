Venturing Crew 413

        Our goal is to provide our community with strong young adults that are always prepared. Young adults involved in Venturing are going to aquire skills in many different activities, set an example for younger Cub Scouts and Boy Scouts, take leadership roles, and most importantly have fun. Our venturers are like police officers in their punctualness and like military officers in their fearlessness. Despite that, they still have a lot of fun!
