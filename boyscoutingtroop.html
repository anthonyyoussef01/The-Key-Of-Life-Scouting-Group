Boy Scouting Troop 413

        Our mission is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and the Scout laws. We strive to deliver a robust and enriching BSA program so that each Scout can achieve meaningful personal growth and character development. We prepare Scouts for success as leaders and role-models in their academic, personal and professional lives, and help Scouts build good character through physical, mental, and spiritual growth. Boy Scouts offers an outdoor program that enhances self-reliance and respect for the environment, and instills in young people the life-long values of ongoing community service.
